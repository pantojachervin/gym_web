Our Story

Forged in a
Garage.
Built for the World.

FITNESSFRONTIER started in 2012 in a 400 sq ft garage in Manila. Two competitive powerlifters, frustrated by imported gear that bent, broke, and bankrupted them, decided to build their own barbell from scratch using locally-sourced aerospace steel.

That first barbell took six months, fourteen prototypes, and one very patient welder named Remy. It still lives in our founder's home gym today — perfectly straight, perfectly knurled, perfectly functional.

We've grown to 50,000+ athletes worldwide, but the obsession hasn't changed. Every product that leaves our facility is checked by hand, stress-tested beyond what any human could apply, and carries the same promise: if it ever fails, we fix it. No receipts. No excuses.
